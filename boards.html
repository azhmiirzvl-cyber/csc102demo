Welcome! This Page is to display the program objectives of a robotics engineering major.

ROBOTICS ENGINEERING DEGREE OBJECTIVES 1. Design and complete robotic and embedded systems solutions that address real-world situations and challenges. 2. Demonstrate embedded microprocessor system and circuit skills. 3. Develop mechanical control systems by implementing transducers, actuators, feedback, vision and sensing systems, and other mechanical systems into robotic platforms. 4. Examine and assess a variety of applications within the field of robotics. 5. Model, analyze, and design systems or processes that integrate hardware and software to control autonomous mechanical systems. 6. Implement artificial intelligence and data systems into robotics
